Our Purpose & Mission

Project Green Sweep is a student-led, action-driven initiative focused on leading all kinds of environmental projects that make a real difference, right now. What makes us stand out is our commitment to hands-on work: we don’t just talk about change, we get out there and make it happen.

Education Campaigns

Through our education campaigns, we visit schools and classrooms to inspire the next generation of environmental leaders. Our team teaches students how small, everyday actions can make a big difference, nurturing eco-friendly habits and empowering youth to protect the planet.

Cleanups

Our cleanup programs target local parks, rivers, and communities to maintain clean, safe environments. Join us in removing litter, improving habitats, and preserving the beauty of nature.

Fundraising

Our fundraisers help power our cleanups, education campaigns, and outreach efforts. They also allow us to give back to other environmental causes. Every donation supports action, not just awareness. Want to make a difference? Support us below and fuel the movement.
